Career and Employment Counselling

Career and employment counselling is designed to help individuals achieve their career and employment goals, and to maximize their potential in the workforce or through unemployment. It provides a supportive and collaborative environment in which an individual can explore their career options and make informed decisions. Our propositions range from career assessments, job search strategies, resume and cover letter development, interview preparation, and skills training. This will be inclusive of negotiating job offers and transitioning to a new job or career.

Categories

Preparing for employment
    Preparing for employment involves developing the skills, knowledge, and resources needed to succeed in a chosen career path. This may include obtaining education or training, building a professional network, and gaining practical experience through internships or volunteer work. Preparing for employment can help individuals feel more confident and competent in their career pursuits and increase their chances of success.
Skill development training and support
    Skill development training and support is a form of training that helps individuals develop new skills or improve existing ones. This type of training may include workshops, classes, or on-the-job training programs that focus on specific skills relevant to a particular job or industry. Skill development training and support can help individuals increase their employability, improve their job performance, and advance in their careers.
